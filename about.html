Introduction

The Big Bang Theory (also abbreviated as TBBT) is an American sitcom created by Chuck orre and Bill Prady, both of whom serve as executive producers on the show along with Steven olaro. All three also serve as head writers. The show premiered on CBS on September 24, 2007.[] The ninth season premiered on September 21, 2015.

The show is primarily centered on five characters living in Pasadena, California: Leonard ofstadter and Sheldon Cooper, both physicists at Caltech, who share an apartment; Penny, a aitress and aspiring actress who later becomes a pharmaceutical representative, and who lives cross the hall; and Leonard and Sheldon's similarly geeky and socially awkward friends and co-orkers, aerospace engineer Howard Wolowitz and astrophysicist Raj Koothrappali.
